### **KEY CONCEPTS** (From Lesson)

#### Electric Force on a Charge in an Electric Field:
> **F = qE**
>
> Where:
> - **F** = Electric force on the charge (N)
> - **q** = Charge magnitude (C)
> - **E** = Electric field strength (N/C or V/m)

#### Direction Rules:
- **For positive charges (+q):** Force direction is **same** as E field direction
- **E field lines:** Directed **away from positive charges** and **toward negative charges**
