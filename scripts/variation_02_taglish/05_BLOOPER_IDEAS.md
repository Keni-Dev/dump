## üí° TIPS

1. **Never stop recording** - Best bloopers happen when akala mo tapos na
2. **React genuinely** - Fake laughter = halata
3. **Include everyone** - Rotate sino ang "biktima"
4. **Save EVERYTHING** - Kahit boring, baka gold pala later
5. **Get consent** - Sure na lahat okay lang sa bloopers
