7. **Content-first is THE consumer-mobile default (items 32, 33, 34, 35, 37, 59, 60).** Pat Walls quote: "your first MVP isn't code, it's content." Warm-up protocol 3-7 days BEFORE posting. Iterate hooks, not the product, daily 14-30 days. ONE viral video (≥50K views with build-this comments) = validation event.

8. **In-app shareable moments matter post-launch (item 37).** Lyttle: 1-in-5 share rate = 20% organic download lift. Design ONE screenshot-worthy moment as the only natural pause in the app loop (game-over screen, top-score card, achievement, reward visualization). Pre-scheduled reply templates + automated first-hour engagement. Rest in 6-hour blocks. Founder cannot reply to every comment in real-time for 72 hours solo — engineer help OR pre-template.

12. **2026 compliance triggers (items 55, 56, 57).** Apple Guideline 3.1.2 toggle-paywall ban (2026-01) — side-by-side or timeline only.
